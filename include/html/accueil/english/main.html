Welcome to phpTournois !!

phpTournois is a tool under GNU/GPL licence design to assist the organization of various tournaments, more specialized in the game tournaments in LAN or online. The integration of Matchmod (dJeyL.net) and AdminBot-MX (neXen.org )makes it possible to launch and recover directly the Counter-Strike matches on the servers with desired maps and parameters.
